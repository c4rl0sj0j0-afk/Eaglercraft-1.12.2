About

A collection of Eaglercraft offline builds. Eaglercraft is an open-source port of Minecraft that runs entirely in the browser, developed by lax1dude. This site bundles multiple historical versions so you can download a zip, unpack it, and run Minecraft locally — or just hit Play above to jump straight into 1.9.
